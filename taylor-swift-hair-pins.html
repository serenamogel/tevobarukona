Shop Taylor Swifts Crystal Hair Pins 2024

June 14, 2024
blog

Taylor Swift took “meet me at midnight” to an astrological level on New Year’s Eve. The singer sported a Clio Peppiatt sequined dress with celestial bobby pins and, of course, Travis Kelce by her side. Remember when bobby pins were all the rage in updos? Like barrettes, accented bobby pins added a little something extra to your look—a fun hair accessory, if you will. Well, Swift rang in the new year with a slicked-back bun adorned with crystallized stars and a crescent moon bobby pin. Safe to say, she’s bringing the look back.

Jennifer Behr 12-Piece Supernova Bobby Pin Set

Jennifer Behr 12-Piece Supernova Bobby Pin Set

The $425 Jennifer Behr 12-piece supernova set from Saks Fifth Avenue is made of Swarovski crystals and handmade in New York. Seeing that Swarovski anything is out of most people’s budget, we found the next best thing that’ll upgrade your hairstyle. Anthropologie sells the Astria Hairpin Set in gold for $58 — note that the item will be available for purchase around January 15, 2024, so mark your calendars now! If spending over $50 on bobby pins is still over your price range — understandably so—then you can snipe an Amazon dupe.

Beaupretty Vintage Star Moon Hair Clips

Beaupretty Vintage Star Moon Hair Clips

The Beaupretty Vintage Star Moon Hair Clips run for only $9.99 and will come within two days for Amazon Prime members. Similar to Taylor’s, the set comes with two snowflake-like stars and a show-stopping crescent moon — and you can choose them in gold or silver! In case you were wondering, no, the bobby pins do not just have to fit in for a New Year’s Eve celebration. You can style them for weddings, nights out on the town, or long-awaited celebrations. Swift was pictured with the bobby pins in a group photo with Patrick Mahomes, Brittany Mahomes, and new love interest Travis Kelce (who truly rocked those indoor shades, if you ask us). The pop star styled her extra sparkles with more silver jewelry, a red manicure, red lip, and winged eyeliner. Just once in our lives, we’d like to browse through Taylor Swift’s closet and accessories — just once!
