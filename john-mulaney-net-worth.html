John Mulaney (born August 26, 1982) is an American actor, comedian, writer, and producer. He is best known for his work as a writer on Saturday Night Live (SNL) and as a standup comedian. He is the creator and star of the Fox sitcom Mulaney. The series is a semi-autobiographical series about his life as a standup comedian and premiered on October 5, 2014. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
