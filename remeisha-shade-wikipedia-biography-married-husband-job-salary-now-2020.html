Remeisha Shade is a retired meteorologist in the Fort Worth area of Dallas for NBC 5.

She was an outstanding meteorologist during her tenure on NBC 5. Many were left to wonder over her abrupt exit from the station after a disagreement with an audience.
In September 2018, she began serving as a Licensed Broadcast Meteorologist at WEWS-TV in Cleveland.

Childhood :

Remeisha was raised in Huntsville, Alabama, where she attended Madison County public high school. She was a cheerleader and through this competition she established her appreciation of the camera.


A twister with winds of about 250 mph ripped through her small town on May 18, 1995, ruining the house of her relatives.
She enjoyed science anyway, but this twister instilled a desire for her to learn how the weather operated.

Job Timeline : Salary & Net Worth

Shade took a position at 12 News KBMT out of Beaumont, Texas, as a meteorologist and reporter in September 2003, a few months after she graduated. There, along with her usual weather analysis, she did field updates and news segments.


She quit KBMT in July 2006 and moved to Alabama to work with WAFF Television as a Broadcast Meteorologist.
Before moving back to Texas to work at KXAS-TV, NBC 5, she worked there for over four years.
