Overall Meaning

Amy Winehouse’s song “Half Time” is about the power of music and how it can consume and uplift us. The opening verse describes how the simple guitar and bass set the foundation for the song, but it’s when the beat kicks in that everything falls into place and overpowers her. She can’t help but dance and feel the rhythm flooding her heart. Winehouse references the ability of music to provide a different perspective, or “consider the change,” during halftime. The second verse describes how the melody feeds her soul, tears her apart, and swallows her whole. She then reminds listeners to recognize the gift of music and how it's stronger than anything else, providing uplift no matter the situation.
