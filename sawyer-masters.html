Sawyer (Japanese: ドリバル Drival) is a character introduced in Pokémon Masters EX. He forms a sync pair with Honchkrow. The sync pair can be obtained through the limited sync pair scout, and was first available on July 30, 2021.

Trivia

  • Along with Rachel & Umbreon, Sawyer & Honchkrow are the first non-5★ sync pair to be featured in a limited-time sync pair scout.
    • Along with Rachel & Umbreon, they were also the first fair-exclusive Dark-type sync pair.
  • In his sync pair story, Sawyer reveals that he's owned Honchkrow ever since it was a Murkrow.
