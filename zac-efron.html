Zachary David Alexander Efron is an American actor and singer. He is a professional, well-trained actor and singer who is known for his outstanding talents. He rose to fame after appearing in the late 2000s for his leading role as Troy Bolton in the ‘High School Musical Trilogy’ (2006 to 2008).

For his performances, Zac Efron got huge acclaim in his career life and now is admired by everyone including fans, viewers, and critics.

Career

Zac Efron started his acting career in the early 2000s and started appearing in movies and series including the musical film Hairspray (2007), the comedy film 17 Again (2009), and many more that are worth watching.

Since his prominence, Zac Efron’s most popular roles in the movies are New Year’ Eve (2011), The Lucky One (2012), The Paperboy (2012), Neighbors (2014), and many others.

Among many, Dirty Grandpa (2016), Baywatch (2017), and The Greatest Showman (2017) are some of the biggest hits of the time.

Additionally, Zac Efron appeared in Ted Bundy’s Extremely Wicked, Shockingly Evil and Vile (2019).

Achievements

In 2021, Zac Efron was awarded the Daytime Emmy Award for the Netflix web documentary series named ‘Down to Earth with Zac Efron’ . It was started in 2020 and ended in the year 2021.
