Career

Draxler made his Bundesliga debut against Hamburger SV on 15 January 2011. He was the fourth-youngest Bundesliga player ever at the time and the youngest in Schalke’s history.  One week later, in Schalke’s 1-0 victory against Hannover 96, he became the Bundesliga’s second-youngest field player after Nuri ahin.
Draxler made his debut for Germany’s under-21 team on August 9, 2011, scoring in a 4-1 victory against Cyprus in a 2013 UEFA European Under-21 Championship qualifier.

On May 26, 2012, he made his senior debut in Germany’s 5-3 defeat to Switzerland, coming on as a substitute in the 62nd minute to replace Lukas Podolski. On June 2, 2013, he scored his first international goal in a friendly against the United States. The goal came in the 81st minute, bringing the score to 3-4, after a bounce off goalie Tim Howard.
