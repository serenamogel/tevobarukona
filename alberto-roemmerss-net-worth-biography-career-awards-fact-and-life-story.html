Alberto Roemmers Net Worth: Alberto Roemmers is a German-native businessman and current resident of Argentina who has a net worth of $2.3 billion. Alberto Roemmers is the head of Laboratorios Roemmers, the largest pharmaceutical group in Argentina. By 1961, the company witnessed a major international expansion as it has already set a firm network of distribution that spanned across 17 countries in Latin America. Today, Laboratorios Roemmers is considered as a leader in terms of unit sales and turnover in the pharmaceutical market in Latin America. Aside from generating big profits, the company has also donated a considerable amount of products to hospitals and state health. Plus, it has awarded grants to over 1,000 research projects to date.
