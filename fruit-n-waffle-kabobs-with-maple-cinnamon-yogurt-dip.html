Fruit ‚Äòn Waffle Kabobs with Maple Cinnamon Yogurt Dip

Fruit ‚Äòn Waffle Kabobs make for a fun breakfast OR dessert!  Dip the kabobs in a super tasty maple cinnamon dip made with Greek yogurt!

Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword fruit waffle kabobs Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 10 minutes minutes Servings 4 Calories 291kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Fresh fruit cut up
  • 16 Eggo Homestyle Minis waffles
  • 1/2 cup Chobani Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp maple syurp
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  • Prepare waffles according to package instructions.

  • Alternate waffles and fresh fruit on a skewer or straw.

  • Combine yogurt, syrup, and cinnamon and serve along side kabobs.
